Author(Excellent Graduation Design)

Transformer-based hyperspectral image classification system

Dec 2021 – Jun 2022 QingDao
  • A new Skip Dense Connection Transfomer (SDFormer) network is proposed, in which the parameter transfer between each encoder in Transfomer is improved to transfer the shallow information of adjacent spectra to the deep one.
  • Extensive experiments have shown that the SDFormer achieves a good accuracy improvement with faster convergence compared to conventional networks previously applied to hyperspectral classification and Transfomer-based networks.
  • Based on these works, a structured design of the model is adopted to control different structures of the SDFormer network by different parameter settings, and the plug-and-play of the relevant modules is realized.
  • A web-side system is built to visualize the model and interact with the front and back ends, and a complete hyperspectral classification system based on Transfomer is constructed.
